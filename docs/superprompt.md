# Smart Avenue - Superprompt & Requirements

## Core Objective
Create a dynamic, world-class international premium retail website for "Smart Avenue" in Patna, Bihar, India. The goal is to design and implement a luxury shopping experience that brings international retail standards to the city.

## Brand Identity
- **Aesthetic**: International, Premium, Aspirational (similar to Marks & Spencer, Westside, or Zara).
- **Tagline**: "Smart Avenue: Where Luxury Meets Convenience."
- **Focus**: Mall-like ambiance, premium brands, smart amenities, and customer convenience. Avoid cluttered "sale-heavy" local designs.

## Key Features
- **Hero Section**: Immersive banner with high-end lifestyle imagery and clear CTAs.
- **Interactive Departments**: Fresh Mart (Grocery), Fashion Studio, Tech Zone, Home & Living.
- **Smart Club**: Loyalty program with tiered benefits (Silver, Gold, Platinum).
- **Gallery**: Masonry grid showcasing store interiors and products.
- **Weekly Offers**: Dynamic display of current deals and a downloadable catalog.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Content management UI for updating offers, gallery, and store settings.
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first design for a seamless experience on all devices.

## Design Constraints
- **Color Palette**: Deep Emerald Green (`#064e3b`), Rich Gold (`#d4af37`), and stark white/black accents.
- **Typography**: Inter (Body) & Playfair Display (Headings).
- **Effects**: Glassmorphism, smooth micro-animations, and high-definition imagery.
